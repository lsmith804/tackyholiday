Porch Beans

Porch Beans are named for the best location to enjoy these. A lot of things taste better outside in the fresh air. Porch beans are recommended during the fall and winter months when the air is crisp and light. Enjoy a bowl of Porch Beans in 10 minutes or less!

You will need:

  • 1 can Campbell's Pork & Beans
  • 2 Oscar Meyer bun-length Weiners
  • 3 tablespoons molasses
  • 1/4 cup water

Steps:

  1. Add water to small skillet pan.
  2. Place hotdogs in pan and heat on medium. Cook to desired doneness. Replace water if necessary.
  3. In a separate small pot, heat beans on medium.
  4. Stir in molasses.
  5. Stir until just bubbling. Remove from heat.
  6. Remove hotdogs from heat. Slice into 1/2 inch chunks.
  7. Add hotdogs to beans and serve.

Martineau Noodles

Martineau Noodles were first imagined in the early 2000s in central Virginia. This simple meal is sure to please friends and family of all ages. Pair with sugar-free fruit juice for an authentic feel. All measurements are dependent on the cook's personal preferences.

You will need:

  • Thin spaghetti
  • Generous amounts of grated parmesan
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Heat water to boiling (use enough water to contain desired spaghetti servings).
  2. Add spaghetti and cook until tender.
  3. Remove spaghetti from heat and drain.
  4. Add butter, salt, and cheese.
  5. Enjoy!
